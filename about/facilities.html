Our Facilities

State-of-the-art infrastructure for world-class filmmaking.

  1. Home
  2. About

Wansekonds Entertainment boasts cutting-edge facilities designed to support every stage of the filmmaking process. Located in Hollywood, our campus provides filmmakers with the tools and environment needed to bring their visions to life.

Sound Stages

Our facility includes multiple sound stages of varying sizes, equipped with the latest lighting grids, acoustic treatments, and support infrastructure. These versatile spaces can accommodate productions ranging from intimate character pieces to large-scale action sequences.

  • Stage 1: 20,000 sq ft
  • Stage 2: 15,000 sq ft
  • Stage 3: 10,000 sq ft (Green Screen Cyclorama)

Post-Production Suites

Our comprehensive post-production wing features dedicated suites for editing, color grading, sound mixing (including Dolby Atmos capabilities), and visual effects. Equipped with industry-standard software and hardware, our suites provide a seamless workflow for finishing your project.

  • 4K/8K Editing Bays
  • Color Grading Theaters
  • ADR and Foley Stages
  • VFX Workstations

Equipment Rental

Wansekonds Entertainment maintains an extensive inventory of high-end cameras, lenses, lighting, grip, and sound equipment available for rent. Our experienced technicians ensure all gear is meticulously maintained and ready for your production needs.

Access the latest technology from leading manufacturers like ARRI, RED, Sony, Cooke, Zeiss, and more.

Additional Amenities

Our campus also includes production offices, dressing rooms, screening rooms, and catering facilities to support your entire crew.
