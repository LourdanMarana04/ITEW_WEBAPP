Marvel Studios Unveils 'The Fantastic Four: First Steps' Trailer

Published on May 6, 2025

Marvel Studios has released the trailer for The Fantastic Four: First Steps, introducing audiences to the MCU's rendition of Marvel's First Family.

Set in a retro-futuristic 1960s, the film follows Reed Richards (Pedro Pascal), Sue Storm (Vanessa Kirby), Johnny Storm (Joseph Quinn), and Ben Grimm (Ebon Moss-Bachrach) as they confront the cosmic entity Galactus (Ralph Ineson) and his herald, the Silver Surfer (Julia Garner).

Amidst this looming threat, the team navigates personal challenges, including Sue's unexpected pregnancy, adding emotional depth to their journey. Directed by Matt Shakman and produced by Kevin Feige, the film is slated for release on July 25, 2025.

"Marvel’s First Family is in excellent hands — the trailer teases a visually stunning, emotionally resonant space adventure."

Marvel.com

The trailer has already generated massive excitement across social media, giving fans a first look at the bold art direction, dynamic chemistry among the cast, and the cosmic scale of the narrative.

Stay tuned to Wansekonds Entertainment for more updates and exclusive looks as the film approaches its highly anticipated debut.
