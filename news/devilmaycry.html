Devil May Cry Animated Series Premieres in April 2025

Published on September 28, 2024

Capcom and Netflix have officially announced that the long-awaited animated adaptation of the Devil May Cry series will premiere in April 2025. The announcement was made with a brand-new teaser trailer, exciting longtime fans of the stylish action franchise.

The series is helmed by Adi Shankar, known for his work on Netflix’s Castlevania, and produced by Studio Mir. The show promises to deliver fast-paced action and a story that stays true to the beloved video game franchise’s roots. Dante, the iconic demon hunter, returns to slash his way through demonic threats in a brand-new storyline exclusive to the series.

"This is a dream project. Dante's story deserves this level of animation and care."

-Adi Shankar

The teaser features glimpses of stylish combat, brooding demon designs, and slick visuals that mirror the energy of the games. With a multi-season plan hinted by the producers, Devil May Cry fans may be in for an expansive journey.
