Fear Street Part Two: 1978

A chilling summer at Camp Nightwing turns deadly in this slasher sequel.

  1. Home
  2. Films
  3. Fear Street Part Two: 1978

Synopsis

In the summer of 1978, Camp Nightwing becomes the site of a gruesome massacre when a counselor is possessed by the witch Sarah Fier's curse. As the body count rises, campers from rival towns Shadyside and Sunnyvale must band together to survive the night. This second installment in the Fear Street trilogy delves deeper into the dark history that has plagued Shadyside for centuries.

Details
