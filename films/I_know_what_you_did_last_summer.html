I Know What You Did Last Summer (2025)

A new generation faces the haunting consequences of a deadly secret, with help from survivors of the past.

  1. Home

Synopsis

After five friends are involved in a fatal car accident, they decide to cover up the incident to avoid consequences. A year later, they find themselves stalked by a mysterious figure who knows their secret. As the terror escalates, they seek help from Julie James and Ray Bronson, survivors of the original Southport Massacre, to confront the vengeful killer and their own guilt.
