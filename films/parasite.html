Parasite

A dark comedy about class divide.

  1. Home
  2. Films
  3. Parasite

Synopsis

A poor family schemes to infiltrate a wealthy household with unexpected consequences, revealing the harsh divisions between social classes.

Details

  • Genre: Thriller, Drama, Dark Comedy
  • Release Date: May 30, 2019
  • Runtime: 132 minutes
  • Language: Korean (with English subtitles)
  • Production Company: Barunson E&A, CJ Entertainment

Main Cast

  • Song Kang-ho as Kim Ki-taek
  • Choi Woo-shik as Kim Ki-woo
  • Park So-dam as Kim Ki-jung
  • Lee Jung-eun as Moon-gwang (the housekeeper)
  • Jang Hye-jin as Chung-sook (Kim family mother)

Supporting Cast

  • Jeong Ji-so as Park Da-hye (Park family's daughter)
  • Lee Sun-kyun as Park Dong-ik (Park family's father)
  • Cho Yeo-jeong as Choi Yeon-gyo (Park family's mother)
  • Park Myung-hoon as Park Geun-sae (the former housekeeper's husband)

Crew

  • Director: Bong Joon-ho
  • Writer: Bong Joon-ho, Han Jin-won
  • Producer: Bong Joon-ho, Dooho Choi
  • Music: Jung Jae-il
  • Cinematography: Hong Kyung-pyo
  • Editor: Yang Jin-mo
  • Production Design: Lee Ha-jun
  • Costume Design: Choi Se-yeon
