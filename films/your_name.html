Synopsis

Your Name (Kimi no Na wa) is a visually stunning Japanese animated film that tells the story of Mitsuha and Taki, two teenagers from very different worlds who mysteriously begin to swap bodies. As they adapt to each other's lives, a profound connection forms between them one that defies the constraints of time and space. Their bond is tested when a looming disaster threatens everything, and they must find a way to meet before it's too late.
