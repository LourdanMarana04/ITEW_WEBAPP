Synopsis

The Big Bang Theory is a long-running sitcom centered on a group of socially awkward scientists and their experiences navigating friendship, love, and pop culture. With sharp wit and nerdy charm, the show follows Sheldon, Leonard, Penny, and their quirky friends as they blend science with everyday life in hilariously unexpected ways.
