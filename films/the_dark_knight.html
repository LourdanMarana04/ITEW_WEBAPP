Synopsis

Batman faces the Joker, a criminal mastermind bent on chaos in Gotham. As the Joker's attacks become increasingly violent, Batman is pushed to his limits, forcing him to question his own principles and the price of justice.

Details

  • Genre: Action, Crime, Drama
  • Release Date: 2008
  • Runtime: 152 minutes
  • Production Company: Syncopy, Warner Bros. Pictures
  • Language: English

Main Cast

  • Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne / Batman
  • Heath Ledger as The Joker
  • Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent / Two-Face
  • Maggie Gyllenhaal as Rachel Dawes
  • Michael Caine as Alfred Pennyworth
  • Gary Oldman as James Gordon

Crew
