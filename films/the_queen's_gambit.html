The Queen's Gambit

The price of genius in the world of chess.

  1. Home

Synopsis

Orphaned at a young age, Beth Harmon rises to become a chess prodigy while battling addiction. Set against the backdrop of Cold War America, her extraordinary talent for chess and her complex relationships propel her to the top of the competitive chess world. As she navigates fame, substance abuse, and the pressures of her genius, Beth must confront her inner demons and find a balance between her identity and her ambition.
