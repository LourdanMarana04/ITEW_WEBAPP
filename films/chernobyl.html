Synopsis

A dramatized retelling of the Chernobyl nuclear disaster of 1986, following the efforts of scientists, firefighters, and the Soviet government to manage the catastrophe.

Details

  • Genre: Drama, History, Thriller
  • Release Date: May 6, 2019
  • Runtime: 60 minutes per episode (5 episodes)
  • Production Company: HBO, Sky UK
  • Language: English, Russian
