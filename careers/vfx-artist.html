VFX Artist

Location: Cabuyao City, Laguna | Type: Full-time

About the Role

Wansekonds Entertainment is seeking a talented and passionate VFX Artist to join our growing visual effects team. You will collaborate with directors, supervisors, and other artists to create stunning visual effects for our feature films and episodic content. This is an exciting opportunity to work on high-profile projects in a creative and dynamic environment.

Responsibilities

  • Assist in creating high-quality visual effects such as compositing, tracking, and simple rotoscoping tasks.
  • Work under the guidance of senior VFX artists to achieve the desired visual style and effects for each project.
  • Contribute to shot clean-up, matte painting, or basic CG integration using available assets.
  • Learn and apply feedback quickly to improve technical and creative output.
  • Help maintain an organized workflow by following established file naming and shot-tracking practices.
  • Stay curious and engaged with evolving VFX tools, techniques, and trends in the industry.

Qualifications

  • Basic experience with VFX or compositing software such as Nuke, After Effects, or Blender (through school, internships, or personal projects).
  • Portfolio or demo reel showing foundational VFX work (student projects are welcome).
  • Understanding of visual storytelling, color, lighting, and composition principles.
  • Willingness to learn from feedback and adapt to team workflows.
  • Good problem-solving attitude and attention to visual detail.
  • Ability to manage time and stay organized when working on multiple tasks.
  • Strong communication and teamwork skills.
