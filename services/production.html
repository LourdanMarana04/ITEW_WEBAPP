Comprehensive Production Management

Wansekonds Entertainment offers end-to-end production services, guiding your project from initial concept development through principal photography and wrap. Our experienced team manages every detail, ensuring a seamless and efficient production process.

We specialize in feature films, television series, commercials, and digital content, adapting our approach to meet the unique needs and scale of each project. Whether you need full production support or specific services, we provide the expertise and resources to achieve outstanding results.

Key Areas of Expertise

Pre-Production

Script breakdown, budgeting, scheduling, casting, location scouting, crewing, and securing permits.

Principal Photography

On-set management, coordination with all departments, ensuring adherence to schedule and budget, technical supervision.

Logistics & Support

Equipment rental coordination, travel and accommodation arrangements, insurance, and production accounting.
